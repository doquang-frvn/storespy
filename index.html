FAQ

Common questions

Does it work on all Shopify stores?
StoreSpy works on the vast majority of Shopify and WooCommerce stores, plus 30+ other platforms like Gearlaunch, ShopBase, and Senprints. A small number of heavily customized headless setups may return partial data. Product listing requires the store's public /products.json endpoint to be enabled.
Can store owners tell I'm analyzing their store?
No. StoreSpy only reads publicly visible data that any visitor sees in their browser. It makes no server-side requests to the store's admin and leaves no trace.
Why is Facebook Pixel ID sometimes missing?
StoreSpy uses an early-injection hook plus live network monitoring to capture pixel IDs loaded via GTM or third-party apps. If a pixel is still not detected, wait for the page to fully load and use the "Rescan Pixels" button. Server-side or encrypted pixels are rare (<5% of stores).
How does the Pro plan work?
Pro is a monthly subscription via Polar.sh. After subscribing, open the extension's Settings, enter the email you purchased with, and confirm the activation code sent to your inbox. No password or license key to manage.
I switched computers — how do I get Pro back?
Just enter the same email you used to purchase in the extension's Settings on the new device. Your subscription is tied to your email, not the device, so there's nothing else to transfer.
Is my browsing data stored anywhere?
No. All scan data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. StoreSpy does not transmit any browsing data to external servers. The only network request verifies your Pro subscription status.
Do you offer team or agency pricing?
Yes — for teams that need multiple seats, unlimited history, or bulk export, contact us directly for custom pricing tailored to your usage.
